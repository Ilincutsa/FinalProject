  • J.K. Rowling

    J.K. Rowling was born on July 31, 1965 in Yate, England and she attended the University of Exeter. She published the first Harry Potter book in 1997 and finished the series in 2007. She published under the name of J.K. Rowling instead of Joanne Rowling due to people being less likely to read if they knew she was a woman writer. Rowling won the Costa Book Award in 1999 for her Harry Potter series and she's still writing today. A few years ago, the Cursed Child screenplay came out where she collaborated with a few other authors to make a book about Harry Potter's child. She also wrote Fantastic Beasts which are currently being made into movies.

  • Emily Suvada

    Emily Suvada was born in Australia and studied math and astrophysics in college. She started writing books later on and published the first book of the 'This Mortal Coil' trilogy in 2017. That book earned her the Oregon Spirit Book Award and many others. Suvada's gone on to write several books as well.

  • S.E. Hinton

    S.E. Hinton was born on July 22, 1950 in Oklahoma. She wrote 'The Outsiders' when she was 17 years old and published it in 1967. The book was based on real life events happening around her, and it became very popular, selling over 4 million copies in the United States.
